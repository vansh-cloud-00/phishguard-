// CORE CONCEPT

What is Phishing?

Before you can defend yourself, you need to understand the enemy.

🎣 Simple Definition

Phishing is a cyber attack where criminals pretend to be someone you trust — your bank, your boss, Google, Amazon — to trick you into handing over sensitive information like passwords, OTPs, credit card numbers, or personal details.

Think of it like a fisherman casting a hook with fake bait. The "bait" is a fake email, fake website, or fake phone call that looks 100% real. When you click or respond, you've been caught.

The scary part? You won't even know it happened until your bank account is empty or your social media is hijacked.

🎣 Deception-Based Attack 🔑 Steals Credentials 💻 Targets Everyone 🧠 Social Engineering
📧

EMAIL PHISHING

Fake emails that look like they're from your bank, Netflix, or Amazon. They create urgency: "Your account will be suspended!" and link to fake websites.

📱

SMISHING (SMS)

Fake text messages asking you to verify your OTP or click a link. Common in fake parcel delivery scams and fake bank alerts.

🌐

FAKE WEBSITES

Websites that look identical to real ones (Google, SBI, HDFC) but steal your username and password the moment you type them in.

// IMPACT ANALYSIS

Why Phishing is Dangerous

One wrong click can ruin everything. Here's what's at stake.

🕵️

IDENTITY THEFT

Attackers steal your name, Aadhaar, PAN, and personal info to open fake credit cards, take loans, or commit crimes under your identity. Recovering from identity theft takes years.

💸

FINANCIAL FRAUD

Once they have your banking credentials or OTP, they drain your accounts in minutes. Many victims lose their entire life savings before they realize what happened.

🔒

FAKE LOGIN PAGES

Pixel-perfect copies of real websites capture your password. Your browser won't warn you because it looks exactly real. Even tech-savvy people fall for these.

📤

DATA LEAKAGE

Your private photos, emails, and company secrets get stolen and sold on the dark web. One employee clicking a phishing link can expose an entire company.

How Phishing Looks in Real Life

These are examples of actual phishing tactics. Learn to spot them.

🚨 FAKE BANK EMAIL

SBI ALERT SCAM

From: noreply@sbi-alerts-verify.com

Dear Customer,
Your SBI account has been SUSPENDED. Click below to verify immediately:

→ http://sbi-verify-now.tk/login

⚠ Red flags: Unofficial domain, urgency language, suspicious link

🚨 FAKE OTP SMS

OTP SMISHING

HDFC-BANK: Your account shows suspicious activity. Verify your OTP immediately:

Your OTP is: 847291

Share with our agent: +91-99XXX-XXXXX

⚠ Red flags: Real banks NEVER ask you to share OTPs

🚨 FAKE LOGIN SITE

GOOGLE CLONE

URL: https://google-accounts-verify.info

[Looks exactly like Google login]

● Username: ____________
● Password: ____________
[Login Button]

⚠ Red flags: Domain isn't google.com, check the URL carefully
